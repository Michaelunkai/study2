Puzzle is an AI-powered knowledge base builder that helps teams and customers access information quickly and easily. It organizes and curates knowledge, making it searchable and accessible through a user-friendly interface. Puzzle is ideal for businesses looking to improve internal communication, enhance customer support, and streamline information management. This tool uses AI to ensure that knowledge bases are up-to-date, relevant, and comprehensive, providing users with the information they need when they need it. Puzzle's efficient knowledge management capabilities make it a valuable resource for any organization.
