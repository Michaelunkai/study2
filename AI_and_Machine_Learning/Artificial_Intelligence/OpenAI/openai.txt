Provider: openai
OpenAI is a leading provider in the AI space, requiring an API key to access its various models. It supports a broad range of models, including popular options like GPT-3.5 and newer iterations. OpenAI also recognizes the OPENAI_API_KEY and OPENAI_MODEL environment variables, offering flexibility in model selection and integration. Additionally, it supports custom URLs with the --url flag, allowing developers to direct their API requests to specific endpoints. This provider is renowned for its powerful and versatile AI capabilities, suitable for a wide array of applications, from natural language processing and content creation to complex problem-solving and data analysis.
