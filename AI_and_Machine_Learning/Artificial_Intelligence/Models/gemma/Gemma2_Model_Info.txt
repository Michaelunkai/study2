Gemma2 is a model developed by Anthropic, a company focused on creating safe and interpretable AI systems. Gemma2 is part of the Claude family, which includes other models like Claude 1 and Claude 2. These models are designed to be large-scale language models that can understand and generate human-like text, similar to models like GPT-3 and GPT-4 by OpenAI.
Gemma2 is used for various natural language processing tasks such as text completion, summarization, translation, and more. The focus of Anthropic in developing models like Gemma2 is on safety and interpretability, ensuring that the models behave in predictable and controllable ways.
