Top 10 Free AI Browser Extensions:
1. Grammarly: Enhances writing with grammar, spelling, and style suggestions.
   - Grammarly for Chrome: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/grammarly-for-chrome/kbfnbcaeplbcioakkpcpgfkobkghlhen
2. Wordtune: Offers advanced rewriting suggestions to improve sentence structure and tone.
   - Wordtune: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/wordtune/jnlgnkpocblljjbeocglmaoehidfckhi
3. QuillBot: Helps with paraphrasing, summarizing, and grammar checking.
   - QuillBot: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/quillbot/ildngcbkhfihhnnhlgnbhfpcilcfnklf
4. Jasper: Provides AI-generated content suggestions for writing.
   - Jasper: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/jasper/gigmlnibkgghkmfhhfnkkjodegpdgijj
5. Ginger: Another writing assistant offering grammar and spell checks, sentence rephrasing, and translation.
   - Ginger: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/ginger-writer/ffnlmbbboaoehlefnkodbefafonokcbd
6. SciSpace Copilot (formerly Typeset): Helps understand academic papers and provides summaries.
   - SciSpace Copilot: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/scispace-copilot-formerly/kkoojmibdhakabfkffopbdekhgfopjlb
7. Otter.ai: Transcribes and summarizes meetings and conversations in real-time.
   - Otter.ai: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/otterai-transcription/koicbdkfhfafjglmlobcceaaliboblkf
8. Scribe: Automatically generates step-by-step guides and documentation from your workflows.
   - Scribe: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/scribe-document-automatio/nfhklnigfmfmifnpmfplhbjomefjlcpj
9. Crystal: Analyzes personality from social media profiles to provide communication tips.
   - Crystal: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/crystal-know-anyone-bette/iikcmcdafcenjfaplmjmgcldnfghbgld
10. Compose AI: Speeds up writing by providing AI-generated text completion and suggestions.
   - Compose AI: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/compose-ai/okjmmhgcbmmfjjloffdfplabjcnniinp
