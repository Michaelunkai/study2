Krisp is an AI-powered noise cancellation app that enhances the quality of audio in real-time by removing background noise, sounds, and echo. Ideal for remote workers, podcasters, and anyone engaged in online meetings, Krisp works across various platforms, including Zoom, Skype, and Microsoft Teams. The app uses advanced algorithms to filter out unwanted sounds, ensuring clear and professional communication. Krisp's ability to provide a quiet environment significantly improves the experience of virtual interactions, making it a valuable tool for maintaining productivity and professionalism in any setting.
