Nanonets is a no-code AI platform focused on document processing and automation. It excels at extracting and analyzing information from unstructured data sources, such as emails, support tickets, and databases. Businesses can use Nanonets to automate repetitive tasks, optimize supply chains, and streamline accounting processes by leveraging historical data. The platform's ability to handle a wide range of documents makes it versatile and valuable for improving operational efficiency. Nanonets offers a free trial for analyzing starter models, making it accessible for businesses to test and integrate its capabilities.
