QNAP (Quality Network Appliance Provider) is a Taiwanese company that specializes in providing network-attached storage (NAS) solutions for home, small/medium business, and enterprise environments. Their products include a wide range of NAS systems, networking devices, and surveillance solutions. QNAP NAS devices are known for their high performance, scalability, and comprehensive software features, such as data backup, file sharing, virtualization, multimedia management, and remote access capabilities. They often run on a proprietary operating system called QTS, which is designed to provide a user-friendly interface and support various third-party applications through a built-in App Center.
