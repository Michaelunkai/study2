### KNIME
**KNIME: Explained**
KNIME (Konstanz Information Miner) is an open-source platform for data analytics, reporting, and integration. It enables users to create data science workflows through a visual programming interface that allows for the assembly of data processing steps in a graphical manner. KNIME integrates a variety of data sources and supports numerous data transformation and analysis tools. Its modular nature and extensive range of nodes (processing units) allow for complex data manipulation, mining, and visualization. KNIME is widely used for machine learning and data mining, providing a powerful and flexible environment for researchers, data scientists, and business analysts to perform detailed data analysis.
