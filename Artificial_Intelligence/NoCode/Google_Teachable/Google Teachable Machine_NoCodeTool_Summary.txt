Google Teachable Machine is a user-friendly no-code AI tool that enables users to train machine learning models using images, sounds, and poses. This web-based platform is perfect for educators, students, and hobbyists looking to experiment with AI without needing extensive coding knowledge. Users can easily create, test, and deploy models for various applications, such as recognizing bird species, classifying sounds, or estimating poses. Google Teachable Machine leverages transfer learning techniques to make model training accessible and straightforward, supporting a wide range of creative and educational projects.
