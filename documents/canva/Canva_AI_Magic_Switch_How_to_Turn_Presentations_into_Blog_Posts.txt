How to Turn Presentations into Blog Posts with Canva AI
Canva has an AI feature called "Magic Switch" that transforms presentations into blog posts. Here's how to use this feature:
1. Sign up for Canva Pro and log in to Magic Studio (you can try it for free for a month).
2. Open your presentation in Canva.
3. Click on "Resize & Magic Switch" in the top toolbar.
4. Select "Transform into Doc" and then choose "Blog Post".
5. Within seconds, it will generate a draft blog post based on your presentation.
